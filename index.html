Today, let’s start with a quote and story.

“You’ve gotta dance like there’s nobody watching, Love like you’ll never be hurt, Sing like there’s nobody listening, And live like it’s heaven on earth.”

This quote has inspired me so much that I don’t have any words to explain it.

In my college days, I was afraid of being judged by people. And to get out of my comfort zone, I started doing internships.

There, I met a person who changed my thinking. She was skinny due to her hormonal issues. But she was one of the kindest people I ever met.

While working with her, I noticed that she never judged people. Instead, she tried to understand them.

One day, I asked her, “How come you’re never afraid of being judged by people? How come you handle it so easily?”
